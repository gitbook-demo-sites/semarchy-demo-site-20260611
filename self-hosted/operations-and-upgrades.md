# Operations and upgrades

Self-hosted readers want confidence more than breadth.

## Suggested areas

- Upgrade sequencing and prerequisites
- Release cadence by supported track
- Rollback and recovery expectations
- Support and escalation points
- Operational observability and log review

{% hint style="warning" %}
If Semarchy's publishing workflow is memory-intensive today, releases and upgrades are exactly where a lighter authoring and publishing model will matter most. These are high-change, high-consequence pages.
{% endhint %}
